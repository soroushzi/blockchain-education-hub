Comprehensive Web3 Roadmap

This roadmap is the result of years of my experience and research in the world of blockchain and NFT. No matter your level, this path is designed for you.

1. Foundational Concepts (Phase One: Understanding the Digital World)
  • Blockchain: What is blockchain?, concepts of decentralization and immutability, consensus algorithms, types of blockchain.
  • Non-Fungible Token (NFT): What is NFT?, the difference between an NFT and a digital file, the existential mission of NFT, technical operation, the "Minting" process.
2. Technical Standards & NFT Evolution (Phase Two: Technical Details & The Future)
  • Foundational Ethereum standards: ERC-721, ERC-1155.
  • Emerging standards: ERC-404, xNFT (Executable NFTs), iNFTs (Intelligent NFTs).
3. NFT Applications (Phase Three: Understanding the Ecosystem)
  • Art and collectibles, video games (GameFi), metaverse, music and cinema, decentralized finance (DeFi), tokenizing physical assets, digital identity and credentials.
4. Metaverse Fundamentals (Phase Four: Expanding the View)
  • What is the metaverse?, foundational features (persistence, interoperability, decentralized economy), technological pillars, monetization models.
5. Practical Entry into the NFT World (Phase Five: Practical Steps)
  • Wallet preparation, buying digital currency, working with NFT marketplaces.
6. Success & Risk Management in NFT (Phase Six: Market Intelligence)
  • Project research and evaluation, investment strategies, common threats and scams, capital management.
7. Play-to-Earn (P2E) Gaming (Phase Seven: Specialization in GameFi)
  • Definition of P2E, tools and platforms, identifying credible games, analyzing economic models, managing digital assets.
8. Creating and Launching an NFT Project (Phase Eight: Becoming a Creator)
  • Pre-creation checklist, project storytelling (NFT Storytelling), team and management, NFT properties, economics and pricing, marketing and launch.
9. Continuous Growth & The Future (Phase Nine: Long-term Vision)
  • The future of NFT, convergence with emerging technologies, educational resources.
